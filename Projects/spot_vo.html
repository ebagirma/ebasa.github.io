Project Introduction

Welcome to an exciting journey where the digital and physical worlds seamlessly converge. In this project, we delve into the captivating realms of robotics and computer vision. Our two-part guide will lead you through the process of setting up a simulation of the Boston Dynamics Spot robot and implementing ORB-SLAM3-ROS for visual odometry.

Part 1 of our journey is dedicated to configuring the Spot robot simulation using ROS and Gazebo. With prerequisites in place, you'll build a digital twin of the remarkable Spot robot, laying the foundation for immersive exploration in the field of robotics.

Part 2 takes us further into the integration of ORB-SLAM3-ROS, enabling real-time camera and imu trajectory visualization. This project promises an enchanting blend of robotics, computer vision, and virtual exploration, ultimately leading to the exciting prospect of transferring our simulation to the physical Spot robot.
